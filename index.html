why ararat mountains are famous?

Mount Ararat, known locally as Agra-Dag, is the highest point in Turkey. The popular peak is famous not only for its beauty but also for the many stories and legends associated with it, most notably the widely-held theory that it was on Mount Ararat that Noah's Ark landed after the Flood.
